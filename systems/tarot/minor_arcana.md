# Minor Arcana (Ace–10)

This document is part of the Tarot Master Document. It defines the rules for numbered cards (Ace through 10) by suit.

---

## 1. Overview

Minor Arcana cards (Ace–10) have no Figurehead summoning ability. Their effects are purely based on the card’s Suit, Position, and Rank multiplier.

---

## 2. Effect Framework

Each Minor Arcana card’s effect is defined by its Suit’s core purpose (Tarot Master Document, §2.3), scaled by Rank, and modified by Position (Upright or Reversed).

Specific effects for each numbered card will be developed in a future expansion. Until then, the following default rule applies:

> A Minor Arcana card of Rank N produces an effect of magnitude N that aligns with its Suit’s purpose (Upright) or inverted purpose (Reversed). The exact nature of the effect is determined by the game system based on the current context, but shall be roughly equivalent to a temporary Domain change, resource gain/loss, or tactical advantage/disadvantage of appropriate scale.

---

## 3. Ace

Ace cards (Rank 1) have a small but focused effect. They are often used for precise, low‑impact actions.
