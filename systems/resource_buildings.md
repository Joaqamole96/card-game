# Resources and Buildings

This document is part of the Game Rules. It defines resource generation and building mechanics.

---

## ARTICLE 4 – RESOURCES

### §4.1 – Gold

Gold is generated by the Economy Domain, by Elite (Merchants), and by trade. Gold is used for construction, recruitment, and certain card costs.

### §4.2 – Faith

Faith is generated by the Religion Domain, by Elite (Clergy), and by Temples. Faith is used for Wands cards and miracles.

### §4.3 – Card Energy

1. **Card Energy** is a resource generated at a base rate of one (1) per Turn.
2. Figureheads and buildings may increase this generation rate as specified by their respective effects.
3. Playing a card costs Card Energy equal to the card’s Count (Tarot Master Document, §3.2.1).
4. Playing a card consumes zero (0) Ticks.

---

## ARTICLE 5 – BUILDINGS

### §5.1 – Slots

1. Buildings occupy slots. One slot is available for every five hundred (500) units of Population.
2. The total number of slots is calculated at the start of each Turn based on current Population.

### §5.2 – Construction

1. **Cost**: The cost to begin construction shall be paid upfront in Gold (and, if specified, other materials).
2. **Time**: Construction requires a specified number of worker‑ticks. Each worker assigned to construction contributes one (1) worker‑tick per Tick.
3. **Queue**: If a building site is occupied by ongoing construction, any additional construction orders for that site are placed in a pending queue. Upon completion of the current project, the next queued building begins automatically, provided workers are available.

### §5.3 – Effects

Completed buildings provide passive bonuses (e.g., Domain increases, resource generation) or enable actions as specified in their descriptions.
