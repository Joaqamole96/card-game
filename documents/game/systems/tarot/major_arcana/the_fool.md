# The Fool
This document defines the rules for the Major Arcana card **The Fool**.

---

## 1. Overview
The Fool patron multiplies the Minor suit's core purpose with randomness and synergy with other Major Arcana. The Reversed Fool flips it with negative random effects of itself or other Cards it is paired with.

---

## 2. Rank‑Based Chance
For The Fool’s effects, “Rank‑based chance” means a probability equal to (Rank × 10)%, where Rank is the numeric value defined in the Tarot Master Document, §2.2. Thus:
- Ace (Rank 1): 10%
- Each increment of Rank adds 10 percentage points.
- Rank 10 and above: 100%.

---

## 3. Resolution Order
Cards played during the Start of Turn Phase (Core Rules, Article 1, §1.2.1) are declared simultaneously but resolve in an order determined by the game system (typically the order in which they were placed). If a Fool card creates a duplicate, the duplicate is placed immediately after the Fool card in the resolution order.

---

## 4. Duplication (Upright)
If an Upright Fool is played with one or more other non‑Fool Major Arcana cards during the same Start of Turn Phase, the game system makes a roll. If the roll is ≤ (Rank × 10)%, the system randomly selects one such non‑Fool Major Arcana among those played and duplicates it.

The duplicate is played immediately after the Fool card in the resolution order, with the same Position, Rank, and Suit as the original, resolving its effects separately. The duplicate requires no additional Card Energy or resource expenditure beyond the original card’s cost.

**Duplicate Court Cards**: If the duplicated card is a Court card and the Figurehead title it would summon is already occupied by an existing Figurehead under the same Player’s control, or if a title conflict would arise under Leaders Document, Article 7, §7.5.3, the Court card instead resolves as a **Ten** of the same Suit and Position (i.e., Rank 10, non‑Court). Its effect (the multiplied Minor Arcana effect) still applies at the ×10 multiplier normally granted by Court cards, but no Figurehead is summoned. The same rule applies whenever a Player plays any Court card while a Figurehead of that title already exists.

---

## 5. Ace‑Level Reversed Trigger (Reversed Fool)
If a Reversed Fool is played with one or more other non‑Fool Major Arcana cards during the same Start of Turn Phase, the game system makes a roll. If the roll is ≤ (Rank × 10)%, the system randomly selects one such non‑Fool Major Arcana among those played and triggers its **Ace‑level Reversed effect** (i.e., the effect as if its Rank were 1). This triggered effect uses the Reversed version of that Major Arcana’s effect, with magnitude based on Rank 1. Because the triggered effect is treated as Rank 1, it cannot summon a Figurehead (as Court cards require Rank 11 or higher).

---

## 6. Fool’s Suit Effects
Each of the following effects is a “Fool’s [Suit] effect”. The specific buff or debuff is selected randomly from the applicable list defined in Schedule A (Fool Effect Table). The magnitude of the effect equals the Rank of the Fool card.
- **Fool’s Swords**: Gain a random combat buff (magnitude = Rank). If played with one or more other Major Arcana, apply Rank‑based chance to duplicate one of them.  
  *Reversed*: Gain a random combat debuff (magnitude = Rank). If played with one or more other Major Arcana, apply Rank‑based chance to trigger one of their Ace‑level Reversed effects.
- **Fool’s Wands**: Gain a random religion buff (magnitude = Rank). If played with one or more other Major Arcana, apply Rank‑based chance to duplicate one of them.  
  *Reversed*: Gain a random religion debuff (magnitude = Rank). If played with one or more other Major Arcana, apply Rank‑based chance to trigger one of their Ace‑level Reversed effects.
- **Fool’s Cups**: Gain a random culture buff (magnitude = Rank). If played with one or more other Major Arcana, apply Rank‑based chance to duplicate one of them.  
  *Reversed*: Gain a random culture debuff (magnitude = Rank). If played with one or more other Major Arcana, apply Rank‑based chance to trigger one of their Ace‑level Reversed effects.
- **Fool’s Pentacles**: Gain a random economy buff (magnitude = Rank). If played with one or more other Major Arcana, apply Rank‑based chance to duplicate one of them.  
  *Reversed*: Gain a random economy debuff (magnitude = Rank). If played with one or more other Major Arcana, apply Rank‑based chance to trigger one of their Ace‑level Reversed effects.
All buffs and debuffs granted by The Fool persist for the remainder of the current Turn unless otherwise specified in Schedule A.
